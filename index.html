Food Guide

Traffic light reference for Vitamin A, copper, and nickel content — the three dietary factors most relevant to the LYL program.

The goal is reduction, not perfection. The LYL program is not an elimination diet. Focus on removing the highest-burden foods and rotating safer options. Individual tolerance varies — use your symptoms as your guide.

Nickel note: Whole grains and beans are high in nickel but also the best sources of soluble fiber for bile binding. If you are not improving after following the program, consider reducing these temporarily while maintaining fiber intake through other means.
Green — Safe / encouraged
Yellow — Use with caution / limit portions
Red — Avoid on the LYL program
